What is Exclusive Books?

Exclusive Books provides services to schools to aim for higher standards and stand out from the rest of the competition.
We provide schools with carefully chosen specialised books to enhance the range of materials being made available for children.

Our aim is that the span of our topics should be a curriculum in itself including even subjects like medecine, economics, how to create a business, solar energy, the peculiarities of Mars and why we might be able to live there (in line with MARS ONE 2025 mission).

Some FAQs

Here are a few Frequently Asked Questions which may help you understand our processes 
better and repond to some of your queries

Why subscribe?

Subscription to Exclusive Books will give you access to your exclusive books catalogue for you to purchase from. The higher your subscription, the more catalogues you will access i.e. The 'Ultimate Plan' subscriber ill have access to all our catalogues, and more.

How do I order my books?

 Similar to your catalogue, your funds/account and your orders will all be managed online. i.e. you will have your personal login and password to our portal where you will be able to browse through your catalogue and order straight from there. Your payments and funds available wil also be visible.

How often are the catalogues updated?

Catalogues are updated quarterly and you will be able to access the latest available books online.

Can I request a particular book?

Requests for particular books or type of books are only made available to Premium and Ultimate Plan subscribers. Upon your request for books or types, we will seek the best available current titles to find the one(s) which best suit your needs.
